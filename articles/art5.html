At Least 4 Boats Sink During ‘Trump Boat Parade’ in Texas, Officials Say

No injuries were immediately reported at the event, which was taking place on Lake Travis. By Bryan Pietsch and Aimee Ortiz The authorities rescued numerous people from the waters of Lake Travis in Texas on Saturday after at least four boats sank at an event promoted as a Trump Boat Parade, officials said. The Sheriff’s Office in Travis County received “multiple” calls of boats in distress starting at 12:15 p.m. local time, a spokeswoman, Kristen Dark, said.
