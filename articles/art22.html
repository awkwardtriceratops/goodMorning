A Vaccine Breakthrough

But amid the gloom comes some light: A trial by the drug maker Pfizer has returned preliminary results suggesting that its vaccine is 90 percent effective in preventing Covid-19. Even more promising, the timeline — contingent on benchmarks being reached and approval being granted — is quite short. There could be enough of the vaccine for 20 million Americans by January.
