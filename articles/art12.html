How the Internet Viewed the First Presidential Debate: Poorly

“The worst debate of all time,” one journalist wrote on Twitter. By Matt Stevens In these polarized political times, it can sometimes feel as if Americans really cannot agree on anything. But Tuesday’s highly anticipated presidential debate, the first between President Trump and Joseph R. Biden Jr., the Democratic nominee, managed to achieve rare bipartisan consensus: Just about everyone offering commentary online agreed that the debate was an unmitigated disaster.
