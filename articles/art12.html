Tropical Storms Marco and Laura Both Headed to the Gulf of Mexico

Marco is expected to become a hurricane on Saturday and Laura is forecast to produce heavy rain over several Caribbean islands. By Derrick Bryson Taylor Tropical Storms Marco and Laura continued to churn in the Caribbean on Saturday, prompting a wave of warnings and watches for several countries, and leading the governor of Louisiana to declare a state of emergency. Marco on Saturday was about 50 miles west of the western tip of Cuba, with maximum sustained winds of 65 miles per hour, the National Hurricane Center said on Saturday evening.
