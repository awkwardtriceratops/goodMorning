While Her Father Rails Against ‘Idiot’ Scientists, Ivanka Trump Talks Ice Cream

The first daughter is trying to woo the white suburban female voters who have become her father’s demographic kryptonite. By Mark Leibovich FRANKLIN, Wis. — President Trump had just been on Fox and Friends, demanding that his attorney general “act” against his opponent before the election. He had, the day before, called Joseph R. Biden Jr. a “criminal,” Dr. Anthony S. Fauci a “disaster,” government scientists “idiots” and members of the media “real garbage.”
