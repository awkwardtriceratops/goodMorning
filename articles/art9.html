When Trump Vandalizes Our Country

The president should grit his teeth and repeat Hillary Clinton’s line from 2016: “We must accept this result.” By Nicholas Kristof Opinion Columnist As it became clear that she would lose the 2016 election and news organizations called the race for Donald Trump, Hillary Clinton spoke to her supporters. “We must accept this result,” she declared. “Donald Trump is going to be our president. We owe him an open mind and the chance to lead.”
