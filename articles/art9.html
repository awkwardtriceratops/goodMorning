A New Front Opens in the Russia-Ukraine Conflict: Borscht

UKRAINE DISPATCH “A lot of things were taken away from Ukraine, but they will not take our borscht,” said a chef who is leading a drive to recognize the soup as a Ukrainian cultural heritage.
