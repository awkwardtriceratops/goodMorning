Barr the Bad or Rudy the Ridiculous?

The Worst of Trump winners are in! By Gail Collins Opinion Columnist When grad students of the future write dissertations on the worst presidential transitions in American history, I have a feeling 2020 will get a lot of attention. True, nobody seceded from the union. But it’s a bad sign when the president refuses to admit he’s lost re-election, then abruptly fires the defense secretary and a top cybersecurity official.
