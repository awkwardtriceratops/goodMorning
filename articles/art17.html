Trump Took $70,000 in Tax Deductions for Hair Care. Experts Say That’s Illegal.

It’s a small but telling detail in The Times’s exposé on the president’s taxes. By James B. Stewart There were many bombshells in The New York Times’s exposé last week about President Trump’s taxes. He has paid basically zero federal income tax for years. His much-ballyhooed businesses are on the ropes. And that was just the headline. But it was a juicy and seemingly less significant matter that jumped out at me: Mr. Trump spent more than $70,000 on hairstyling during several years of his run on “The Apprentice,” his reality-TV show.
