Meet a Secret Trump Voter

‘Being a lesbian who’s voting for Trump is like coming out of the closet again.’ By Bret Stephens Opinion Columnist Chris is a registered Democrat in her 50s who lives in Manhattan. She’s well-educated, well-traveled and well-informed. She has voted for candidates of both parties over the years and was enthusiastic for Bernie Sanders in 2016. She’s asked me not to publish her last name. It would not go down well for her at the store where she works as a manager if her colleagues knew that she plans to vote for Donald Trump. Chris is also gay. “Being a lesbian who’s voting for Trump is like coming out of the closet again,” she tells me.
