Modern Love Podcast: Devoted but Doomed

What he hadn’t disclosed — to his crush or to anyone at school — was that he was transgender and had transitioned at age 15. But he knew he had to tell this “charismatic acquaintance,” for what they had was flirtatious and unstoppable; it was, as Malcolm put it, physics. As it turned out, his crush had something to share too. She was originally from India, and her parents forbade her from dating anyone who was not Indian. They dated anyway — quietly, both knowing that each day of sweetness together was drawing them closer toward the last. An excerpt from Cecilia’s Tiny Love Story: “We met on a train from Paris to Barcelona. Sitting next to one another, we argued over who could use the power outlet. (‘Sorry, I think it’s mine.’).”
