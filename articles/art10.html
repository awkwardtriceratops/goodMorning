Some Latinos Voted for Trump. Get Over It.

I’m Latina. I still don’t know what that means. By Isvett Verde Ms. Verde is a staff editor in Opinion. On Wednesday the country woke up to the fact that a sizable portion of Latinos liked President Trump enough to help him clinch a victory in Florida. And a CNN exit poll suggested that Mr. Trump picked up more Latino voters in several other key battleground states compared to his numbers in 2016.
