All About Ant Group, the Next Big Tech I.P.O.

The tech giant’s coming share sale will be among the largest ever. But the company has made most of its impact in just one country: China. By Raymond Zhong One of China’s most influential tech companies, the internet finance titan Ant Group, is poised to raise a boatload of cash by selling shares. The sale puts another stamp on China’s importance as a digital powerhouse. But it also shows how the tech world is fracturing.
