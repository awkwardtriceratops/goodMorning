Mark Zuckerberg’s ‘Evolving’ Position on Holocaust Denial

Facebook’s new moves to stop the lies and conspiracy theories seem more random than rigorous. By Kara Swisher Contributing Opinion Writer When Facebook’s founder and chief executive, Mark Zuckerberg, told me two years ago in a podcast interview that Holocaust deniers might not mean to lie, my first instinct was to reply, “That’s completely idiotic.” I managed to stifle myself. In our interview, I let him explain how he wanted to govern the giant social media platform he alone controlled.
