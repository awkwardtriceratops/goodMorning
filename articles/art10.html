Live Updates: Biden’s Transition to the Presidency Formally Begins

Pennsylvania certifies its election results, making Biden’s victory there official. No longer held back, Biden’s teams make contact with defense and other officials. Biden will announce cabinet picks today, as Trump vows to keep fighting the results. The start of the transition means Biden’s battle against the coronavirus begins. Biden is expected to name Janet Yellen for Treasury secretary. She’d be the first woman in the job.
