11 of Our Best Weekend Reads

Black activists in Chicago. Retail in Manhattan. An elephant’s pandemic journey. Bike shorts. And more. By Jillian Rayfield Welcome to the weekend. This week, we had major news in the U.S. presidential race: Joe Biden’s selection of Sen. Kamala Harris as his running mate. As we get closer to the fall, the election season is intensifying, and it will continue to do so over the next two weeks with the Democratic and Republican conventions. So before all of that starts, try to take a break from the news cycle. Maybe make some homemade ice cream. Or enjoy a bit of moth-watching. But still make time for some incredible journalism.
