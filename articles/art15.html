In a Fitting Tribute, the Fed Cup Is Renamed After Billie Jean King

ON TENNIS The newly named Billie Jean King Cup honors a player and coach who has always been equal parts champion, teammate and advocate. By Christopher Clarey Bud Collins, the celebrated tennis commentator and historian, once described the Fed Cup as “a splendid idea with a lame name.” “He did? That’s perfect,” said Billie Jean King, laughing hard. “I wish he were still alive. He would enjoy this.”
