11 of Our Best Weekend Reads

Kamala Harris’s Indian roots. Our new work lives. Greek life during Covid-19. The sounds of New York City. And more. By Anna Schaverien Welcome to the weekend. I hope it brings you some sunshine wherever you are. If there’s no sun in sight, maybe you’ll be inspired by the people who are packing up and temporarily moving to warmer climes. (I have to confess, I’m tempted.)
