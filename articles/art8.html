John McAfee, Software Pioneer Who Fled the Law, Is Arrested in Spain

The U.S. authorities accused the former antivirus impresario of evading taxes and said his extradition to American soil was pending. By Mike Ives John David McAfee, an antivirus software pioneer who fled Belize in 2012 ahead of a murder investigation there, has been arrested in Spain on tax evasion charges, the U.S. Justice Department said on Monday.
