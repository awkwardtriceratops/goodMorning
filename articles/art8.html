The Power of Low-Stakes Productivity

The smaller the better. By Leah Fessler Welcome to the Smarter Living newsletter! Every Monday, we email readers tips and advice for living a better, more fulfilling life. Sign up here to get it in your inbox. Not feeling very productive lately? Good. The coronavirus pandemic has warped our already-warped cultural feelings around productivity — and socially signaling that productivity — convincing us that if we haven’t perfected homemade sourdough, read three new books and written the next great American novel, we have failed quarantine. The solution isn’t practicing “self-care” or slapping on a chia-pudding mask. It’s shifting our focus to small wins.
