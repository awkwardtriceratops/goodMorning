A Timeline of Trump’s Symptoms and Treatments

What is known so far about what happened in the days leading up to the president’s hospitalization. By Christina Morales and Allyson Waller As President Trump continues to be treated at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center, doctors and senior White House staff members have given conflicting updates about the president’s condition, treatments and when he learned he was infected with the coronavirus. A timeline of events about the president’s illness is drawn from his tweets, news conferences, statements from the White House and reporting from The New York Times.
