It Shouldn’t Be This Close. But There’s Good News, Too.

In the long lines of voters, I see hope. By Nell Irvin Painter Nell Irvin Painter is the author of “The History of White People.” My people are wringing their hands and tearing their hair out over the early presidential election returns: How could it be so close? After all we’ve been through, with the lying and the white nationalists and the children in cages and the 230,000-plus dead from Covid-19, how could tens of millions of our fellow citizens still have voted for Mr. Trump?
