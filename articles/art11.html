The Myth of Trump’s Political Genius, Exposed

His victory in 2016, the product of luck as much as skill, went to his head. By Jamelle Bouie Opinion Columnist It sounds counterintuitive, but it’s true: Donald Trump is bad at electoral politics. Yes, he is the president, which by itself would suggest the opposite. But to look at his conduct during the coronavirus pandemic is to see someone who doesn’t understand his own political interests and won’t listen to anyone who does.
