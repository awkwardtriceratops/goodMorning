After 4 Years of Trump, Medicare and Medicaid Badly Need Attention

Science and objective analysis need to be revived. By Peter B. Bach Dr. Bach is the director of the Center for Health Policy and Outcomes at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. President-elect Joe Biden has pledged to “marshal the forces of science” in his administration. Undoubtedly he needs to start by bolstering the credibility of the Food and Drug Administration and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
