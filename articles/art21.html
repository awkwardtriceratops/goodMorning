The Killing of Breonna Taylor, Part 1

When asked about Breonna Taylor, her loved ones paint a picture of a determined, responsible young woman: “One of those people she made a plan and she went — that was it.” As part of an investigation for The New York Times, our correspondent Rukmini Callimachi and the documentary filmmaker Yoruba Richen spent weeks interviewing those who were closest to Ms. Taylor, who was killed in her home by the police in Louisville, Ky., in March. She was 26.
