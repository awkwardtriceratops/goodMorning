The 72nd Emmy Awards will be held tonight. Several winners were announced last week. Compiled by The New York Times Netflix and HBO’s “Watchmen” dominated the 2020 Emmy nominations, which were announced on July 28. The Emmy Awards ceremony will be held tonight and broadcast on ABC beginning at 8 p.m. Eastern. It will be hosted by Jimmy Kimmel.
