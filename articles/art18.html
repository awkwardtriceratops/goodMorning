Everyone Is Gay on TikTok

Straight young men on the app are posting suggestive videos with their buddies. It’s not just about the views. By Alex Hawgood Connor Robinson, a 17-year-old British TikTok star with rosy cheeks and a budding six-pack, has built a large following by keeping his fans thirsty. Between the daily drip of shirtless dance routines and skits about his floppy hair, Mr. Robinson posts sexually suggestive curve balls that, he said, “break some barriers.”
