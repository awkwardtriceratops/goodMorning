This Year From Hell

The Avenue Beat song rings true: “I am kinda done. Can we just get to 2021?” By Susan E. Rice Ms. Rice is a former national security adviser. Finishing up the dinner dishes one evening last week, I was jolted by a song on my teenage daughter’s playlist. In the radio-edited version of “F2020,” Avenue Beat delivers a captivating chorus, followed by: “I am kinda done. Can we just get to 2021?” Turning to my daughter, I joked, “Shouldn’t that be a sort of anthem for this year?” “For many in my generation, it already is,” she replied.
