Walter Lure, Guitarist for a ‘Mythical’ Punk Band, Dies at 71

With the Heartbreakers, a shooting star in New York’s punk rock scene, he was a more proficient guitarist than many of his peers. Then came a very unpunk second act. By Steven Kurutz Walter Lure, who played rhythm guitar for the Heartbreakers, a pioneering band of the 1970s New York punk scene, and who had a most unpunk second career on Wall Street, died on Aug. 21 at in Queens. He was 71.
