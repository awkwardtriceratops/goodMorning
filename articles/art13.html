Name One Good Thing About 2020

We invite readers to think of something positive about a dismal year. Dear readers: Without discussing politics, name one good thing about 2020. Submissions should be no more than 200 words. The deadline is Monday, Dec. 14, at 10 a.m., Eastern time. Email: letters@nytimes.com Please include your name, city, state and contact information, and put “year-end” in the subject line.
