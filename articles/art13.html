‘It Really Was Abandonment.’ Virus Crisis Grips British Universities

Students holed up in Birley hall of residence at Manchester Metropolitan University, where over a thousand students are in an enforced period of lockdown due to high numbers testing positive for Covid-19.Credit...Mary Turner for The New York Times Supported by By Benjamin Mueller LONDON — Inside a dormitory now known by students as H.M.P., for Her Majesty’s Prison, trash piled up in shared kitchens. Students washed their clothes in bathroom sinks. Security guards stalked the gates, keeping anyone from leaving or entering.
