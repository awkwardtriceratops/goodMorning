What Impact Could the U.S. Election Have on Your Country?

Opinion wants to hear the international perspective. By Rachel L. Harris and Lisa Tarchak Ms. Harris and Ms. Tarchak are senior editorial assistants. President Trump “appears determined to use his final 10 weeks in office to pursue a scorched-earth foreign policy that will only make Mr. Biden’s job harder,” write Robert Malley and Philip Gordon in “Trump Still Has 70 Days to Wreak Havoc Around the World.” Do people around the world agree? We’d like to hear from readers living outside of the United States. What are your thoughts on the U.S. election? How are you feeling about the results? And what, if anything, do you think the impact could be on other countries, including your own? You can submit your thoughts in the form below. We may use your response in an upcoming article.
