Trump Funnels Record Subsidies to Farmers Ahead of Election Day

Despite an extraordinary government bailout, America’s agriculture sector remains under severe economic pressure. By Alan Rappeport WASHINGTON — For the American farmers whom President Trump counts on for support, the government money is flowing faster than ever. Federal payments to farmers are projected to hit a record $46 billion this year as the White House funnels money to Mr. Trump’s rural base in the South and Midwest ahead of Election Day.
