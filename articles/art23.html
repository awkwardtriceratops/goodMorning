In Year of Dashed Expectations, Buoying Artists and Educators

THE NEEDIEST CASES FUND An initiative provides cultural programming to the city’s 1,800 public schools and supports arts organizations in the process. By Sara Aridi When the coronavirus pandemic brought New York City to a standstill, New York Community Trust, a foundation that supports local nonprofits, had many concerns. Among them was that an aggressive response to the virus would spell a blow to the economy — and also to public schools, which had a record $447 million investment in arts education for the 2018-19 school year.
