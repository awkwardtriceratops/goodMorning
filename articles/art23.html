N.Y.P.D.’s Top Latino Official Resigns After Clashing With Mayor

Chief Fausto Pichardo told colleagues that he felt insulted by Mayor Bill de Blasio during a turbulent time for the city, a police official said. By Ashley Southall The New York Police Department’s highest-profile Latino official resigned abruptly on Tuesday after complaining to colleagues that Mayor Bill de Blasio had insulted him, two police officials said.
