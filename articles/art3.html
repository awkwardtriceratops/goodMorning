The Best 60-Game Stretch in a Century Belongs to Ichiro Suzuki

No one managed to hit .400 this year despite excited speculation about the possibility in a truncated season, underscoring how impressive Suzuki’s late-season stretch in 2004 was. By Brad Lefton After Charlie Blackmon of the Colorado Rockies completed a stretch of six consecutive multiple-hit games in August, his batting average ballooned to an even .500 after 17 games. Nolan Arenado marveled at his teammate’s otherworldliness.
