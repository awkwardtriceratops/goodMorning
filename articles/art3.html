Modern Love Podcast: When Getting Old Never Happens

When the firefighter chased her down the street to ask her out, she pinned him as “a bald, white, middle-aged New York City cliché.” On their first date, no topic was off-limits. Not racism, not abortion, not substance abuse. With each date, another debate. Today’s episode is about the space they found in each other — and the unexpected aftermath of their breakup.
