UPS Driver Charged in a Series of Shootings on an Oregon Highway

Officials said Kenneth Ayers fired at vehicles from a UPS tractor-trailer on seven separate occasions starting in May. A motive for the shootings, which injured one person, was unknown. By Maria Cramer A driver for UPS was arrested on Thursday in connection with a series of shootings along a highway in southwest Oregon that injured one person and unnerved motorists, officials said.
