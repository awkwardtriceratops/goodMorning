Ruth Bader Ginsburg. Rwanda. Electric cars. Bagels. Lenny Kravitz. And more. By Jillian Rayfield Welcome to the weekend, and welcome to the first week of fall. The weather might be cooling down, but the pandemic means the season’s usual activities have become a bit more complicated. But you can certainly try to partake, like these extremely dedicated fans finding ways to watch live baseball in person. Or just curl up under a new blanket and eat this spinach-potato pie.
