Amanda Seyfried Finally Stakes Her Claim

Seyfried on her Catskills farm, which she bought to remind herself that a set is not home.Credit...Molly Matalon for The New York Times Supported by By Kyle Buchanan The goats didn’t want to go out in the morning, Amanda Seyfried said. There was one in particular that was giving her trouble, the one Seyfried described as the “God-knows-how-big-she-is goat.” This one was spoiling for a fight. This one could not be moved. So what did Seyfried do? She got in that goat shed, planted her feet and pushed. The goat pushed back, of course. That’s the thing about goats: They’re stubborn. They also have horns, and Amanda Seyfried, a 34-year-old actress, does not. It was frustrating. It was exhausting. It was also, Seyfried hastened to add, completely awesome.
