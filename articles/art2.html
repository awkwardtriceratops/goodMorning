What Are the 10 Best Books of 2020? You Can Find Out First

Join The Times for a live virtual announcement of this year’s 10 Best Books list, featuring a celebrity guest. Every year, book lovers look forward to the publication of the 10 Best Books list, chosen by the editors of The Times Book Review. But this year, Times subscribers have a chance to know these titles before other readers do. On the morning of Nov. 23, the Book Review will announce the 10 Best Books of 2020 as part of a live virtual event, ahead of the list’s publication that day.
