Best TV Shows of 2020

Television wasn’t spared by the pandemic, but there were still more great shows than any one person could watch. Here’s a look at the top series of the year, as well as some that signed off for good. By James Poniewozik, Mike Hale and Margaret Lyons JAMES PONIEWOZIK Everyone knows that the year 2020 changed everything. What this 10-best list presupposes is: What if it didn’t? The pandemic, the first and last story of this year, naturally disrupted television — talk shows, sports and especially scripted comedy and drama. My list might have included “The Good Fight,” if its unfinished fourth season had not been left dangling.
