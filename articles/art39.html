Right or wrong, Times/Siena surveys of Arizona have tended to show bigger Biden leads than other surveys of Arizona this cycle. Data for Progress‚Äôs poll reflected the ‚Äú2016 electorate,‚Äù which may make their numbers relatively comparable to national polls. CNN‚Äôs methodological document doesn‚Äôt make it entirely clear how their sample was adjusted to be representative of people who watched the debate.
