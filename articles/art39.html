About 50 years ago, he recalled, Mr. Biden’s dogs were named “Governor” and “Senator.” “These were sizable dogs,” he said. “Just like, apparently, the ones he’s got now.” Former Representative Bob Brady of Pennsylvania, a longtime Biden ally, said that he “didn’t meet Major, but I met Champ,” describing the pet as “gorgeous.” “He was probably throwing something around, it could be a toy,” he said, speculating on Mr. Biden’s injury. “I spoke with his guys. He’s fine, he’s fine.”
